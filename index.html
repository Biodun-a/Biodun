Biography

Abdul wahab is a Nigerian born in warri delta state, know for his strong academic background and dedication to engineering. he holds a bachelors degree in mechanical engineering, which laid a foundation for his deep interest in the field, currently pursuing a masters degree furthering his expertise and aiming to contribute signifiacantly to the engieneering sector

Why I Joined AltSchool Africa

Attraction to Practical Learning:

I chose to join AltSchool Africa because of its commitment to practical, hands-on learning. Unlike traditional academic programs, AltSchool emphasizes real-world applications of technology, ensuring that students are not only learning theoretical concepts but also applying them in meaningful ways. This approach is particularly appealing to me because I believe that true understanding comes from doing, and I wanted to be part of a program that prioritizes this method of education.

Focus on Industry-Relevant Skills:

Another reason for my decision was AltSchool's focus on preparing students for the demands of the tech industry. The curriculum is designed to equip students with the skills that employers are actively seeking, such as software development, problem-solving, and collaboration. Knowing that I would graduate with a skill set that is directly applicable to the workforce gave me confidence that this program would set me on the right path toward achieving my career goals.

Community and Mentorship:

The opportunity to be part of a vibrant community of learners and professionals was also a significant factor. AltSchool’s emphasis on mentorship and collaboration means that I will have the chance to learn from industry experts and network with peers who share my passion for technology. This collaborative environment is essential for my growth, as it provides a support system and valuable insights that will guide me throughout my learning journey.

Goals For Joining School of Engineering Program

Deepening Technical Knowledge:

One of my primary goals in the School of Engineering program is to deepen my understanding of software development. I aim to master the fundamental concepts of programming, algorithms, and system design, which are critical to building robust and efficient software solutions. By engaging with the curriculum and tackling complex projects, I hope to gain a comprehensive understanding of these areas, laying a solid foundation for my future career.

Enhancing Problem-Solving Skills:

Another key objective is to improve my problem-solving abilities. The tech industry is constantly evolving, and engineers are often faced with challenges that require innovative and creative solutions. Through the program, I want to sharpen my analytical thinking and develop strategies for tackling difficult problems. This will not only make me a better engineer but also prepare me for leadership roles where decision-making and problem-solving are crucial.

Gaining Practical Experience:

I also intend to gain as much practical experience as possible. AltSchool’s project-based approach allows students to work on real-world challenges, providing an invaluable opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge in practical settings. My goal is to participate in a variety of projects that will give me hands-on experience, enabling me to build a strong portfolio that demonstrates my skills and versatility to potential employers.

Networking and Community Building:

Finally, I aspire to build strong relationships within the AltSchool community. Networking with peers, mentors, and industry professionals is essential for personal and professional growth. I plan to actively engage in the community, contribute to discussions, and collaborate on projects, all of which will help me to learn from others and share my own insights. This network will be instrumental in my career development, providing opportunities for mentorship, collaboration, and lifelong learning.
